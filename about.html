About

日本語版

Hello — I’m Katy.

I’m a UX designer with 10 years experience across startups, big tech and agencies — London, Hong Kong, Melbourne and now Tokyo.

I gravitate towards tangled processes and problems that only make sense once you see the whole picture — then the collaborative orchestration to make it tangible.

Born and raised in the UK, I have a BSc in Interactive Media Design. I’ve worked with multidisciplinary teams to research, design, test, and ship products — end-to-end, from discovery through delivery.

I speak English natively, Cantonese fluently and Japanese conversationally (JLPT N2).

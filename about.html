About・私について

Hello,

my name is Katy, I was born and raised in the UK.

Growing up fascinated with technology, the emerging internet, art and design. I went to university and graduated with a BSc in Interactive Media Design. Since then, I have lived and worked in a number of countries including London, Hong Kong, Melbourne, Australia and now Tokyo.

I've been a UX Designer in a design studio, multiple start-ups and a large IT corporation. Collaborating with multi-disciplinary teams to plan, design, test, and launch.

I speak native English, fluent Cantonese, Hakka and Japanese (N2, July 2021).

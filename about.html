About

Hello — I’m Katy.

Born and raised in the UK, I’m a UX Designer with a BSc in Interactive Media Design and years of cross-cultural design experience.

I’ve worked in London, Hong Kong, Melbourne, and Tokyo — leading UX across tech startups, agencies, and tech giants.

I’ve partnered with multidisciplinary teams to research, design, test, and ship products that work for real people — end-to-end, from discovery through to delivery.

Living and working across four cities has shaped how I see the world and how I work with others. I speak native English, fluent Cantonese and conversational Japanese (JLPT N2).

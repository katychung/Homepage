About

Hello — I’m Katy.

Born and raised in the UK, I’m a UX Designer with a BSc in Interactive Media Design and over a decade of cross-cultural design experience.

I’ve worked in London, Hong Kong, Melbourne, and Tokyo — leading UX initiatives across startups, design studios, and a global IT corporation.

I’ve partnered with multidisciplinary teams to research, plan, design, test, and launch products that deliver real value for both users and businesses. As a generalist at heart, I enjoy working end-to-end across the process.

I speak native English, fluent Cantonese and conversational Japanese (JLPT N2).
